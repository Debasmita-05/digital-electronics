Overview

All digital circuits fall into one of two fundamental categories based on whether their output depends only on present inputs or also on past states.

Combinational Circuits

Output depends only on current inputs. No memory, no clock.

  • Adders and subtractors
  • Multiplexers and demultiplexers
  • Encoders and decoders
  • Comparators

Sequential Circuits

Output depends on current inputs and past states. Uses clock and memory.

  • Flip-flops
  • Registers and shift registers
  • Counters
  • Finite state machines
